# Career Prep Video Series — Content Plan

Episode roadmap for the [NGO Opportunities YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@NGO-Opportunities), grown out of a WhatsApp community of 500+ members sharing NGO job openings.

## Format for every episode

1. **Hook** (first 3 seconds) — a question or bold claim as on-screen text, to stop the scroll.
2. **Script** — 3–5 short scenes, each with: voiceover line, on-screen text/caption, and a visual suggestion.
3. **Call to action** — one clear next step (e.g. "Comment CV and I'll send you the checklist").
4. **Description/caption** — a short summary plus 3–5 hashtags for YouTube and WhatsApp.
5. **Length** — keep the spoken script under ~180 words so the video stays under 2 minutes.

## Posting cadence

Suggested: 2–3 videos per week, cycling through categories, e.g. Mon = CV/Cover Letter, Wed = Interview Prep by Stage, Fri = Interview Simulation. NGO Career Tips episodes work well as a monthly "extra" drop.
