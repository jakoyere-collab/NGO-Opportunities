About NGO Opportunities

NGO Opportunities grew out of a WhatsApp community of 500+ members sharing genuine NGO job openings — checked and linked straight to the hiring organization, the same way it's always been done by hand. This site continues that, now updated daily, and adds short videos covering the questions members ask most often about CVs, cover letters, and interviews. Both are just the start — more ways to support the community are on the way.
